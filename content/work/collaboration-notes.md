---
title: "Partnering with research to ship faster"
date: 2025-06-04
description: "A lightweight workflow that kept analysis aligned with product goals."
tags: ["collaboration", "research", "process"]
---

On a cross-functional project, timelines kept slipping because research findings arrived too late for planning. I proposed a simple cadence: short discovery memos, early directional reads, and a final synthesis when the data matured.

The change helped both teams move in parallel instead of serially. It also produced cleaner documentation and fewer surprises at launch.
